Welcome to Landmark Companies' Online Property Sales System. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy & Policy page outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website.

1. Introduction about the policies:

We collect and use your personal information in accordance with this Privacy & Policy. By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this policy.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons:

We use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your use of our website. Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. Web beacons are small graphic images that are embedded in web pages or emails. We use cookies and web beacons to improve your user experience, analyze website usage, and personalize content and advertising.

3. Information you provide to us:

We collect personal information that you provide to us when you register for an account, make a purchase, or contact us. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.

4. Information we collect about you:

We also collect information about your use of our website, including your IP address, browser type, device type, and operating system. We may also collect information about your location, preferences, and interests.

5. Use of Data:

We use your personal information to provide you with our services, process your transactions, and communicate with you. We may also use your information to personalize your experience, improve our website, and analyze website usage.

6. Asking us to remove your personal information:

You have the right to request that we remove your personal information from our systems. To do so, please contact us at Landmark@gmail.com. Please note that we may need to retain certain information for legal or administrative purposes.

7. Changes to this Privacy & Policy:

We may update this Privacy & Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates or changes. Thank you for choosing Landmark Companies' Online Property Sales System. If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy & Policy, please contact us at Landmark@gmail.com.
